Place your photos in this repository under the images/ folder and videos under videos/.

Guidance:
- Thumbnails used by the site: images/photo1.jpg, images/photo2.jpg, images/photo3.jpg
- Local video example: videos/video1.mp4

If you used different filenames, open index.html and update the references in the gallery or video tag to match your uploaded filenames.

If your videos are large (>100 MB), consider uploading them as unlisted YouTube videos and replacing the iframe VIDEO_ID in index.html.
